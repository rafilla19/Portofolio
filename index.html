Projects

1001-Events
1001-Events is a web-based platform developed during our second semester with the goal of providing President University students with centralized, comprehensive, and up-to-date access to campus event information. The platform was built using HTML, CSS, PHP, and MySQL, focusing on usability and accessibility to ensure a smooth experience for students looking to stay informed about ongoing and upcoming events.
Learnlytics
Learnlytics is a web-based data science application designed to analyze and predict student academic performance based on their learning activities. This project utilizes data such as grade history, participation in quizzes, assignments, and discussion forums to provide insights for students, lecturers, and academic administrators. The target users of this application include university students, faculty members, and campus management who aim to understand and improve academic outcomes through data-driven approaches.
Projectify
Projectify is a mobile application developed using Dart with Flutter and integrated with Firebase, aimed at providing a centralized platform for computing students to explore, reference, and collaborate on academic projects. Built with additional support from C++, CMake, and minor usage of Swift, and HTML, the app was created to assist the Research and Development division of PUMA IS in fostering technological innovation and peer collaboration.
Transaction Performance Analysis Dashboard
Developed an interactive dashboard using Google Looker to analyze transactional data. Performed exploratory data analysis (EDA), cleaned and validated data, and visualized key metrics such as revenue trends, product performance, and customer behavior to support data-driven decisions.
Expenditure Cycle
This project simulates an Expenditure Cycle System, one of the core components in an Accounting Information System (AIS). It focuses on managing the procurement and payment processes for goods and services within an organization. The system is built using HTML to demonstrate the structure and flow of expenditure activities.
iSense
iSense is a web-based system that recommends the most suitable academic concentration (Data Science or ERP) for Information System students. By analyzing students’ grades, interests, and skills, the system applies a machine learning model (Logistic Regression) to suggest the best path aligned with their strengths and future goals. Built with HTML, CSS, Django, and integrated with a trained ML model, iSense delivers simple and smart academic decision support.
